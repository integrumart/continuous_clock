\# Continuous Clock for NVDA



This add-on provides periodic time announcements and an optional ticking sound every minute to help users stay aware of time.



\## Features

\* \*\*Periodic Announcements\*\*: Automatically announces the current time at user-defined intervals (1-60 minutes).

\* \*\*Minute Ticking\*\*: Plays a subtle beep sound at the start of every minute.

\* \*\*Manual Announcement\*\*: You can assign a custom shortcut to hear the time whenever you need it.



\## Usage

1\. Open the NVDA menu by pressing `NVDA+N`.

2\. Navigate to \*\*Tools\*\* > \*\*Continuous Clock Settings...\*\* to configure intervals and sound options.

3\. To assign a shortcut for manual announcement, go to \*\*Preferences\*\* > \*\*Input Gestures\*\* and look under the \*\*Continuous Clock\*\* category.
